Kill / pivot signal

Not a low number (n=20 can't even tell 10% from 30%). The kill signal is a consistent value objection across replies ("ik zie de meerwaarde niet") — not "stuur maar info". One call reaching a genuine "maybe/ja" = the bet is alive.

4Legal & compliance — the constraints

Mitigate, don't pretend exempt. NL's spam ban (art. 11.7 Tw) is opt-in, channel-neutral, and covers businesses — "only email BV salons" is false comfort. But the real risk isn't a fine (ACM only ever fined bulk spammers) — it's WhatsApp number bans + the GDPR/IP exposure of publicly republishing a salon's photos/team/reviews.

📸 Instagram DMamber

The mitigations (all cheap, do them)

✓ noindex + robots.txt on every mockup done · unguessable slug · ~30-day auto-unpublish · instant takedown

✓ opt-out line on every message done · a 1-page LIA + privacy note · a suppression list · a send-log

✓ a dedicated, warmed, burnable WhatsApp number; ramp 5→10→20/day; rotate at >2–3% block/report

⛔ Do NOT pitch a Treatwell-commission saving. The 35% is only a marketplace new-client fee; Treatwell is also their booking software (direct/repeat already 0%), and our site embeds their Treatwell booking link → it saves zero commission. Claiming a saving is a checkable falsehood. Lead value on brand / Google-vindbaarheid / ownership.

5Reply → call → close runbook

The send is just the trigger; this is where the first €999 is won. (Full copy in docs/OUTREACH.md §4.)

  1. Reply lands → respond same day. A cooling warm reply is the biggest leak. Offer the call as a "samen doorlopen" walkthrough: "Zal ik 'm even met je doorlopen — hoe 't werkt en wat 't kost? 15 min."
  2. The "Treatwell brengt mij klanten" reply (you'll get it a lot): validate → reframe to visitekaartje / Google / van jóu. No commission claim. If they're truly thriving & uninterested, let them go gracefully.
  3. The 15-min call: agenda → walk their live mockup & invite 2 small edits (the real endowment lever) → light true-yeses → price only now (€999 eenmalig, eigen .nl, 5 werkdagen; care plan after) → trial-close.
  4. On a clear "ja": confirm, then send the iDEAL link in the moment (don't "mail it later") → asset-collection list → schedule the build.
  5. Build & deliver in 5 werkdagen → customer #1 becomes your first real social proof (a named local quote beats any vague claim).

7The decision gate (run after every batch)

Bayesian, per channel: posterior Beta(1+positives, 1+N−positives); report P(p>0.10), not a point estimate. Don't pool across channels.

SCALEP(p>0.10) ≥ 0.80 and ≥1 reply reached the call stage → more of the same, accumulate toward ~100–150 sends.
KEEP GOINGPosterior straddles 0.10 and N < ~50 → just thin data; send another batch, no change.
CHANGE MESSAGEGood human-click but P(p>0.10) ≤ 0.20 → opener earns looks, offer/CTA doesn't convert → fix mockup/CTA.
CHANGE CHANNELLow delivered/click across 2 batches, or block/report > 2–3% → rotate number / next channel.
RETHINK PRODUCTOnly after ~100+ sends across ≥2 channels with a consistent value objection — never on early noise.
